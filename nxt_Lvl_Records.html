Nxt Lvl Records is a music label started in 2019 by John Blaze, and aimed to help artist just starting their careers in music. It provided them with a structure and provided assistance with everything from single and album covers ers, music videos, studio time, and registration platforms to get artist paid.
It has worked with artist across the country as well as some overseas artist. Nxt Lvl Records has always believed that artist should be able to work on the art and not have to worry about all the backend of the music busniess.
Although this is just a stepping stone for most artist, even after the seperation from the label, artist continue to grow and excel in the music industry never forgetting where they started out.
